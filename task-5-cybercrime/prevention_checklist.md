# Cyberbullying Prevention & Support
A brief guide to staying safe online and accessing help.
---
## Prevention Checklist
- [ ] **1. Private Profiles:** Ensure all your accounts are set to "Friends only" to control who can view your content.
- [ ] **2. The billboard Test:** Make sure to not post anything you wouldn't want on a giant public sign.
- [ ] **3. Enable Mfa:** Enable Multi-Factor Authentication to prevent account hijacking.
- [ ] **4. Ghost Mode:** Disable location tagging (GPS) on your photos and posts.
- [ ] **5. Don't Retaliate:** It is better to Ignore or block rather than arguing since bullies want a reaction.
- [ ] **6. Unique Usernames:** Use different handles across apps to prevent cross-platform stalking.
- [ ] **7. Save evidence:** Take screenshots of harassment before it gets deleted.
- [ ] **8. Be an upstander:** Report abuse whenever when you see it. Don't be a bystander.

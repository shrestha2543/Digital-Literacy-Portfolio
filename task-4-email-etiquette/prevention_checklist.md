# Cyberbullying Prevention and Support
A concise guide to staying safe online and accessing help.
---
## Social Media Checklist
- [ ] **1. Private Profiles:** Set accounts to "Friends only" to control who can view your content.
- [ ] **2. The billboard Test:** Don't post anything you wouldn't want on a giant public sign.
- [ ] **3. Enable Mfa:** Enable Multi-Factor Authentication to prevent account hijacking.
- [ ] **4. Ghost Mode:** Disable location tagging (GPS) on your photos and posts.
- [ ] **5. Don't Retaliate:** Bullies want a reaction. Ignore or block rather than arguing.
- [ ] **6. Unique Usernames:** Use different handles across apps to prevent cross-platform stalking.
- [ ] **7. Save evidence:** Take screenshots of harassment nefore it gets deleted.
- [ ] **8. Be an upstander:** Report abuse when you see it; don't just watch.
